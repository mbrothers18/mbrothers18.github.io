WxFL Overview

League Overview

WxFL is a fantasy weather league where teams of six CONUS cities (three starters and three bench) will compete against other teams in various meteorological phenomena occurring at their cities over the course of three seasons (Winter, Spring, and Summer). Teams will be ranked against opposing teams in each of these categories for each month as well as their season total for select categories. Points will be awarded (between 0 and 1) based on how close a team is to the leader in each category. The team with the most points (monthly and season) at the end of the season (Winter, Spring, and Summer) will be deemed the winner. The top three teams each season will earn the right to participate in WxFL's postseason, The Gauntlet Playoffs. For further details on the league's rules, see Rulebook.

Scoring Categories

  1. Winter Season (Dec-Feb)
    • Accumulated Snowfall (in)
    • Maximum 24-hr Snowfall (in)
    • Cold Temperature Days (count of days with max temperature at or below 32F plus count of days with min temperature at or below 0F)
    • Severe Weather Days (count of days with tornado, hail, and/or wind reports within 100 miles)

  2. Spring Season (Mar-May)
    • Accumulated Snowfall (in)
    • Accumulated Rainfall (in)
    • Tornado Days (count of days with tornado reports within 50 miles)
    • Severe Weather Days (count of days with hail and/or wind reports within 50 miles)

  3. Summer Season (Jun-Aug)
    • Accumulated Rainfall (in)
    • Maximum 24-hr Rainfall (in)
    • Hot Temperature Days (count of days with max temperature at or above 95F plus count of days with max temperature at or above 100F)
    • Severe Weather Days (count of days with tornado, hail, and/or wind reports within 75 miles)

For results of the current month from all cities in the competition, starting cities for each team, and scores in each category for each team, see Scores. Meteorological data for verificaiton are retrieved from NWS/WFO CF6 Reports and NWS/SPC Storm Reports

The Gauntlet Playoffs

The Gauntlet serves as the postseason championship for the WxFL where teams will be awarded the ability to acquire a certain number of cities based on their performance in the previous three seasons and try to rack up the most “events” out of all teams participating in the playoffs. The Gauntlet Playoffs will begin a few days after the conclusion of the Summer season (~ Sept. 5th) and will run no later than mid October (~ Oct. 15th). The team that observes the most events among it’s cities will become the winner.

Playoff Events

  • First Frost (Temp <= 32F)
  • First 90F Day (Temp >= 90F)
  • First Snowfall > 3 in
  • First Rainfall > 0.5 in
  • First City Accumulated HDD > 75
  • First City Accumulated CDD > 75
  • First Wind Gust > 40 mph
  • First Severe Weather Report within 100 miles (tor>hail>wind)
  • First Thunder Report
  • First Fog
  • Nearest Location of First Hurricane Landfall

For further details on The Gauntlet Playoffs, see Rulebook.
